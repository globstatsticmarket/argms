<h3 data-start=""1101"" data-end=""1145""><strong data-start=""1105"" data-end=""1143"">What Are BOPP Self-Adhesive Tapes?</strong></h3>
<p data-start=""1147"" data-end=""1408"">BOPP self-adhesive tapes are pressure-sensitive tapes made from BOPP film and coated with an acrylic adhesive. These tapes offer excellent resistance to moisture, chemicals, and temperature variations, making them ideal for packaging and sealing applications.</p>
