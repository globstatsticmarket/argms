<h3 data-start=""1163"" data-end=""1210""><strong data-start=""1167"" data-end=""1208"">What Are Semiconductor Gas Purifiers?</strong></h3>
<p data-start=""1212"" data-end=""1496"">Semiconductor gas purifiers are high-precision filtration systems used in semiconductor fabrication plants to eliminate contaminants such as moisture, oxygen, hydrocarbons, and particles from process gases. These gases play a critical role in various manufacturing steps, including:</p>
<ul data-start=""1498"" data-end=""1598"">
<li data-start=""1498"" data-end=""1537""><strong data-start=""1500"" data-end=""1535"">Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)</strong></li>
<li data-start=""1538"" data-end=""1553""><strong data-start=""1540"" data-end=""1551"">Etching</strong></li>
<li data-start=""1554"" data-end=""1573""><strong data-start=""1556"" data-end=""1571"">Lithography</strong></li>
<li data-start=""1574"" data-end=""1598""><strong data-start=""1576"" data-end=""1596"">Ion Implantation</strong></li>
</ul>
<p data-start=""1600"" data-end=""1734"">By ensuring ultra-high purity (UHP), gas purifiers prevent defects in semiconductor chips and enhance overall production efficiency.</p>
