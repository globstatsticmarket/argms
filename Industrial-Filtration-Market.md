<h3 data-start=""1332"" data-end=""1372""><strong data-start=""1336"" data-end=""1370"">What Is Industrial Filtration?</strong></h3>
<p data-start=""1374"" data-end=""1659"">Industrial filtration is the process of <strong data-start=""1414"" data-end=""1472"">removing solid particles, contaminants, and impurities</strong> from <strong data-start=""1478"" data-end=""1512"">air, water, oil, and chemicals</strong> in industrial settings. It ensures that <strong data-start=""1553"" data-end=""1656"">machinery operates efficiently, meets safety standards, and complies with environmental regulations</strong>.</p>
<h3 data-start=""1661"" data-end=""1701""><strong data-start=""1665"" data-end=""1699"">Types of Industrial Filtration</strong></h3>
<ol data-start=""1703"" data-end=""2417"">
<li data-start=""1703"" data-end=""1852""><strong data-start=""1706"" data-end=""1724"">Air Filtration</strong> &ndash; Used in <strong data-start=""1735"" data-end=""1795"">HVAC systems, cleanrooms, and industrial exhaust systems</strong> to remove dust, pollutants, and airborne contaminants.</li>
<li data-start=""1853"" data-end=""2006""><strong data-start=""1856"" data-end=""1877"">Liquid Filtration</strong> &ndash; Ensures <strong data-start=""1888"" data-end=""1923"">clean water, oil, and chemicals</strong> for industries such as <strong data-start=""1947"" data-end=""2003"">food &amp; beverage, pharmaceuticals, and petrochemicals</strong>.</li>
<li data-start=""2007"" data-end=""2123""><strong data-start=""2010"" data-end=""2028"">Gas Filtration</strong> &ndash; Removes contaminants from <strong data-start=""2057"" data-end=""2120"">natural gas, industrial exhaust, and compressed air systems</strong>.</li>
<li data-start=""2124"" data-end=""2262""><strong data-start=""2127"" data-end=""2145"">Oil Filtration</strong> &ndash; Used in <strong data-start=""2156"" data-end=""2210"">automotive, hydraulic, and manufacturing processes</strong> to remove particulates and extend equipment life.</li>
<li data-start=""2263"" data-end=""2417""><strong data-start=""2266"" data-end=""2289"">Membrane Filtration</strong> &ndash; Includes <strong data-start=""2301"" data-end=""2362"">reverse osmosis (RO), ultrafiltration, and nanofiltration</strong> for <strong data-start=""2367"" data-end=""2414"">water purification and wastewater treatment</strong>.</li>
</ol>
