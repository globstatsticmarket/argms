<h2 data-start=""1109"" data-end=""1142""><strong data-start=""1112"" data-end=""1140"">What is PPC Advertising?</strong></h2>
<p data-start=""1144"" data-end=""1311"">PPC advertising is a <strong data-start=""1165"" data-end=""1192"">digital marketing model</strong> where advertisers bid on keywords and pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. The most common forms of PPC include:</p>
<ol data-start=""1313"" data-end=""1839"">
<li data-start=""1313"" data-end=""1421""><strong data-start=""1316"" data-end=""1330"">Search Ads</strong> &ndash; Appear on search engine results pages (SERPs) when users search for specific keywords.</li>
<li data-start=""1422"" data-end=""1520""><strong data-start=""1425"" data-end=""1440"">Display Ads</strong> &ndash; Visual banner ads that appear on partner websites within a display network.</li>
<li data-start=""1521"" data-end=""1629""><strong data-start=""1524"" data-end=""1540"">Shopping Ads</strong> &ndash; Product listings that appear in search results, often used by e-commerce businesses.</li>
<li data-start=""1630"" data-end=""1737""><strong data-start=""1633"" data-end=""1646"">Video Ads</strong> &ndash; Ads that play before or during video content, commonly seen on platforms like YouTube.</li>
<li data-start=""1738"" data-end=""1839""><strong data-start=""1741"" data-end=""1760"">Remarketing Ads</strong> &ndash; Target users who have previously interacted with a brand&rsquo;s website or app.</li>
</ol>
<p data-start=""1841"" data-end=""1967"">PPC is favored for its ability to <strong data-start=""1875"" data-end=""1964"">deliver measurable results, provide targeted audience reach, and offer budget control</strong>.</p>
