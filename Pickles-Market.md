<p data-start=""1215"" data-end=""1445"">Pickling is one of the oldest <strong data-start=""1245"" data-end=""1277"">food preservation techniques</strong>, dating back over <strong data-start=""1296"" data-end=""1311"">4,000 years</strong>.
