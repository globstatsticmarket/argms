<h2 data-start=""1046"" data-end=""1078""><strong data-start=""1049"" data-end=""1076"">What is Particle Board?</strong></h2>
<p data-start=""1080"" data-end=""1332"">Particle board is an engineered wood product that is manufactured by compressing wood particles and resin adhesives. It is a cost-effective alternative to plywood and MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard), widely used in furniture, flooring, and cabinetry.</p>
