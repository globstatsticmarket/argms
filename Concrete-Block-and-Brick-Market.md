<h3 data-start=""1214"" data-end=""1260""><strong data-start=""1218"" data-end=""1258"">What Are Concrete Blocks and Bricks?</strong></h3>
<p data-start=""1262"" data-end=""1535"">Concrete blocks and bricks are <strong data-start=""1293"" data-end=""1329"">essential construction materials</strong> made from a mixture of <strong data-start=""1353"" data-end=""1392"">cement, sand, water, and aggregates</strong>. They are widely used in the construction of <strong data-start=""1438"" data-end=""1473"">walls, pavements, and buildings</strong> due to their <strong data-start=""1487"" data-end=""1532"">durability, strength, and fire resistance</strong>.</p>

<li data-start=""1697"" data-end=""1828""><strong data-start=""1700"" data-end=""1726"">Hollow Concrete Blocks</strong> &ndash; Lightweight blocks with <strong data-start=""1753"" data-end=""1772"">hollow cavities</strong> for <strong data-start=""1777"" data-end=""1825"">better insulation and reduced material usage</strong>.</li>

<li data-start=""2062"" data-end=""2184""><strong data-start=""2065"" data-end=""2084"">Concrete Bricks</strong> &ndash; Small, rectangular bricks made from <strong data-start=""2123"" data-end=""2148"">cement and aggregates</strong>, ideal for <strong data-start=""2160"" data-end=""2181"">wall construction</strong>.</li>
